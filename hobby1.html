Pool

One of my primary pastimes involves participating in and organizing amateur pool tournaments within the local community. Despite having only engaged in this activity for a year, it's become a significant and dedicated pursuit for me. I am committed to achieving excellence in the game. Pool, being an immensely humbling sport, demands a substantial investment of time, unwavering consistency, and a great deal of patience.

I have livestreamed some of the tournaments that I have hosted on YouTube and Facebook.
